UZH-EF

The UZH-EF battery comprises three tasks for each of the executive functions inhibition, shifting, and updating.

  • How to Cite

Background

The UZH-EF battery was part of a larger cognitive assessment battery used in a study investigating the impact of bilingualism on cognitive ability. The tasks were modelled after the work by Miyake et al. (2000) and originally programmed in Java-based Tatool by Claudia von Bastian, Alessandra Souza, and Miriam Gade at the University of Zurich.

How to Cite

von Bastian, C. C. Souza, A. S., & Gade, M. (in press). No evidence for bilingual cognitive advantages: A test of four hypotheses. Journal of Experimental Psychology: General. doi: 10.1037/xge0000120
